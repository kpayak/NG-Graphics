About Us.
We deal in block making and CTP plates which are used for banners and newspaper advertisements.

CTP

Computer-to-plate (CTP) is an imaging technology used in modern printing processes. In this technology, an image created in a Desktop Publishing (DTP) application is output directly to a printing plate.

Experience

We have over 25 years of experience in developing CTP (violet) plates, film processing and block making. Our pre-press house is known for quality, commitment & timely delivery.

Customers

Our customers range from offset printing houses to leading newspaper agencies across MP. We have a 5-star reputation within our customers. Feel free to contact us to get references.

How we work ?
Very simple..

Send us an e-mail with attached picture of the poster that you want to convert to a plate. This can be in .jpeg or .png format.

Give us 1-2 business days to process the picture. You can always call us or check the status of your job online.

Once we have processed the plate for your image, we will ship it out to you for free! You should recieve your plate within 1-2 business days.
